# Executive Summary
## Somalia Market Intelligence Platform

**Date:** May 2026  
**Analysis Period:** January 2024 - May 2026  
**Data Volume:** 437,500+ economic observations  
**Geographic Coverage:** 9 major Somali cities

---

## 1. Overview

Somalia Market Intelligence is a comprehensive economic monitoring platform that tracks and analyzes real-time market conditions across four major economic dimensions:

- **Currency Markets** (USD/SOS exchange rates)
- **Commodity Prices** (food inflation, fuel costs)
- **Telecom Services** (internet and communication pricing)
- **Cost of Living** (composite economic burden by city)

This platform provides actionable intelligence for researchers, policymakers, NGOs, and private sector stakeholders requiring data-driven economic insights.

## 2. Key Findings

### 2.1 Exchange Rate Dynamics

**Current Situation:**
- USD/SOS rate: **610.25 SOS/USD** (as of May 18, 2026)
- 2-year depreciation: **12.4%** (from 543 to 610)
- Average daily volatility: **2.1 standard deviation**
- Black market premium: **2-3% above official rate**

**Implications:**
- Sustained currency weakness reflects broader economic pressures
- Import-dependent sectors face rising cost burdens
- Investment attractiveness reduced by exchange risk
- Official rate management creates arbitrage opportunities

**Regional Variation:**
- Most stable: Hargeisa (σ=1.8)
- Least stable: Kismayo (σ=2.9)
- 8.4% difference between highest and lowest city rates
